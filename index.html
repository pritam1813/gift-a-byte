Food Distribution in Assam

During the recent flood in Assam, our organization worked to distribute food and other necessary supplies to those affected by the disaster. We recognized the urgent need for assistance and knew that we had the resources and capabilities to make a difference.

Covid Relief Activities

Our donations helped to provide nutritious meals to those who may have otherwise gone without, and reduced the burden on organizations working to feed those in need during this difficult time. We are proud to have been able to contribute to these efforts and make a difference in our community.

Providing Food to Local Public Schools

We donated a variety of non-perishable food items, including canned goods and dried goods, to the school. These items were distributed to students and families in need, ensuring that they had access to nutritious meals during this challenging time.
